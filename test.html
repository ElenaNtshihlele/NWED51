About

This site helps students and staff report and find lost items on campus. Cards, phones, keys, and IDs are reunited faster when details are shared in one trusted place.

Frequently asked questions

How do I update a report?

Submit the form again with the new details, or email the team with your reference information.

Can I add an image after submission?

Yes. Email lostandfound@gmail.com with the image attached and the item name in the subject line.

What if I find something valuable?

Use Report found, keep the item secure, and consider notifying campus security for high-value property.

Campus tips

  • Keep your student ID and keys in a dedicated pocket or pouch.
  • Check listings daily if something went missing after class.
  • Label belongings with your name or a reachable email where appropriate.
