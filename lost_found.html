About

This site helps Sol Plaatje University students and staff report and recover lost items on campus. Cards, phones, keys, and IDs are reunited faster when details are shared in one trusted place.

The system fetches supplementary listings from the JSONPlaceholder REST API, demonstrating real asynchronous JavaScript with fetch(), error handling, and live DOM updates.

Frequently asked questions

How do I update a report?

Submit the form again with updated details, or email the team with your reference information.

Can I add an image after submission?

Yes — email lostandfound@spu.ac.za with the image attached and the item name in the subject line.

What if I find something valuable?

Use Report found, keep the item secure, and consider notifying campus security for high-value property.

How long are listings kept?

Listings are reviewed monthly. Items unclaimed after 90 days are donated or disposed of as per campus policy.

Campus tips

  • Keep your student ID and keys in a dedicated pocket or pouch.
  • Check listings daily if something went missing after class.
  • Label belongings with your name or a reachable email where appropriate.
  • Take a photo of valuables before you bring them to campus.
